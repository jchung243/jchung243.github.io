TEMPERATURE

This visualization depicts temperature in Fahrenheit versus latitude. As you can see, the trend suggests that the temperature is higher closer the datapoint is to the latitude 0 (the equator). The tightness of the pattern suggests that there is a fairly strong relationship between temperature and latitude.

This particular dataset utilized 632 random points from around the globe and was taken on March 16th, 2019. You can find the data used to plot this visualization here.
